I DID THE EXTRA CREDIT!!!!!!!

If you move the Sun, the Earth and Moon follow, while still doing their orbits

Controls:
X = Move Sun in positive x direction
X + Shift = Move Sun in negative x direction

Y = Move Sun in positive Y direction
Y + Shift = Move Sun in negative Y direction

Z = Move Sun in positive Z direction
Z + Shift = Move Sun in negative Z direction

R = Reset Sun's position back to the origin (0.0, 0.0, 0.0)
